The Review Loop

Every label should become evidence we can reuse.

  1. InviteGive a reviewer a scoped, private entry point.
  2. OrientTeach the scene decisions and the evidence standard.
  3. InspectLet the reviewer switch between the original and inspection grid.
  4. DecideCapture the label, confidence, image quality, and a short note.
  5. PromoteUse checked decisions to build the benchmark and find failure modes.

“The repeatable data factory is the first product.”

Applications become more valuable as the review and benchmark loop gets stronger.
